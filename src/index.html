The data we are collecting will be used to help us plan our new museum in Smithfield. Some of the findings of this evaluation may be shared with third parties such as the City of London Corporation and the Greater London Authority who are funding the new museum project. Once evaluation is complete, all raw data collected will be securely destroyed. The views expressed are the views of the public only and are not endorsed by the museum.
